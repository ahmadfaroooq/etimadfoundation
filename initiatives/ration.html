Ration Bag Breakdown

Every item carefully chosen to sustain a family through Ramadan.

Each ration bag is designed to support a family for the entire month of Ramadan with essential staples. In 2025, we distributed 120 ration bags to families in Lahore and Okara. Our 2026 goal is to reach 130+ families — ensuring no household goes without during the blessed month.
