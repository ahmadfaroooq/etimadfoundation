About Us

Serving humanity with compassion and transparency.

Our Mission

Etimad Foundation was born from a simple but powerful conviction — that every person deserves dignity, especially in times of need. Operating across Lahore and Okara, we channel the spirit of Ramadan into meaningful action: feeding families who might otherwise go without, and building community around shared iftar tables.

We operate with full transparency, ensuring every rupee donated reaches the people it was meant for. Our volunteers and donors form a circle of trust — etimad — that grows stronger each year. We don't just distribute food; we restore hope, one meal and one family at a time.

Our Story

How a small idea became a movement.

What started as a group of young people distributing a handful of iftar meals on a single street in Lahore has grown into something far bigger than any of us imagined. The name Etimad — meaning trust — was chosen deliberately. We wanted to build something our donors, volunteers, and the families we serve could rely on completely.

Year after year, that circle of trust has expanded. More volunteers show up. More families are reached. More meals are shared. We've learned that when you operate with sincerity and keep your promises, people notice — and they want to be part of it. That organic, word-of-mouth growth is the truest measure of our success.

Our Journey

Key milestones that define our path.

2024

The Seed Was Planted

A small group of friends came together with a shared vision — to serve those who needed it most during Ramadan. The first iftar meals were distributed in Lahore.

2025

Scaling With Purpose

We served 15,000+ iftars and distributed 120 ration bags across Lahore and Okara — proving that a grassroots effort can create real, measurable impact.

2026

Our Biggest Year Yet

With your support, we're aiming to feed 25,000+ people at our Iftar Dastarkhuwan and deliver ration bags to 130+ families. The mission keeps growing.
