About Us

Serving humanity with compassion and transparency.

Our Mission

Etimad Foundation was founded on a simple belief — every person deserves dignity, especially during difficult times. Across Lahore and Okara, we transform the spirit of Ramadan into action by supporting families with essential food supplies and community iftar initiatives.

We are committed to ensuring that every donation directly benefits those in need. Through structured planning and volunteer-driven execution, we aim to deliver meaningful impact with honesty and care.

Our Vision

We envision communities where generosity becomes a shared responsibility. Our goal is to expand sustainable food programs, empower youth volunteers, and build a trusted network of donors united by transparency.

By strengthening trust — etimad — we aim to grow our reach every year and ensure no family is left behind.

What We Do

Turning generosity into measurable impact.

Ramadan Ration Drives

Distribution of essential grocery packages to families facing financial hardship, ensuring they can observe Ramadan with dignity and peace of mind.

Community Iftar Programs

Organized collective iftars that foster unity, gratitude, and a sense of belonging across neighborhoods.

Transparent Fund Management

Clear tracking of donations to maximize direct impact while minimizing administrative overhead.

Commitment to Transparency

Accountability is the foundation of Etimad. We maintain structured fund tracking and open communication with donors to ensure clarity at every stage.

When trust grows, so does impact. Transparency strengthens generosity, enabling us to serve more families each year.
