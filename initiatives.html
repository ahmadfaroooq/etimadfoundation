Our Initiatives

Two core programs driving our Ramadan mission forward.

Iftar Dastarkhuwan

We set up community iftar tables across Lahore and Okara, serving wholesome meals to thousands of fasting individuals every Ramadan. Last year, we fed over 15,000 people.

Learn More

Ration Bag Distribution

Each carefully assembled ration bag provides a family with essential food supplies for the entire month of Ramadan. In 2025, we supported 120 families with full ration bags.
